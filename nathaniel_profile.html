Bio/About me

I was born in Peoria, Illinois on November 30, 1999 and am currently majoring in computer information systems (CIS) at Bradley University. Some of my interests include video games, film/TV shows, and music.

  • The Yakuza series
  • Hades
  • Apex Legends

Video games are a fun subject because you can enjoy them for a variety of different reasons, whether it be the gameplay itself, the story, the (if present) multiplayer mode, the graphics/artstyle, the soundtrack, or any combination of those reasons.
